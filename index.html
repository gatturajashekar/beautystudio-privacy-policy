2. Information We Collect

The App collects the following categories of personal information:

  • Full name

Your personal information is processed solely to provide salon appointment services, manage bookings, send confirmations and reminders, operate the loyalty program, prevent misuse, and improve service reliability. We do not use your data for automated profiling or automated decision-making.

We process personal data based on user consent at registration, performance of services requested through the App, legitimate business interests necessary to operate securely, and compliance with applicable legal obligations.

5. Loyalty Program Information

The App includes a promotional loyalty system. One hundred (100) coins are equal to ₹1 for redemption purposes. Coins are awarded only after completed bookings and may be reversed for cancellations, incomplete services, misuse, or technical error. Coins are non-transferable, non-withdrawable, and have no independent cash value outside the App.
