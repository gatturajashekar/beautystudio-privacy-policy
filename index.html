2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Full name
  • Phone number (used for secure authentication via OTP)
  • Age range (13–120, optional)
  • Gender (optional)
  • Booking history and selected services
  • Loyalty and referral records
  • Device push notification token (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
  • Technical log data such as request metadata processed by secure cloud infrastructure

We do not collect payment card details, biometric identifiers, precise location data, or sensitive financial information through the App.

3. Technical and Log Information

The App operates on secure cloud infrastructure including Firebase Authentication and Amazon Web Services (AWS) such as AWS Lambda, DynamoDB, API Gateway, and CloudWatch. These systems may automatically process limited technical metadata such as request timestamps, device type, and system logs for security monitoring and service reliability. This information is used strictly for operational and security purposes.

4. Purpose of Processing

Your information is processed solely to:

  • Enable secure user authentication
  • Manage salon bookings
  • Send booking confirmations and reminders
  • Operate the loyalty rewards system
  • Prevent misuse or fraud
  • Maintain system security and reliability

We do not use your data for advertising profiling, behavioral tracking, or automated decision-making.

5. Legal Basis

We process personal data based on user consent at registration, performance of requested services, legitimate business interests for secure operations, and compliance with applicable laws including India’s Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

6. Loyalty Program

One hundred (100) coins equal ₹1 for redemption within the App. Coins are issued only after completed bookings and may be reversed in cases of cancellation, misuse, or technical correction. Coins are non-transferable and have no cash value outside the App.

7. Notifications

The App uses Firebase Cloud Messaging to send booking-related notifications only. We do not send promotional marketing notifications. Users may disable notifications through their device settings at any time.

8. Data Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Cloud databases use encryption at rest. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel through role-based access controls. Administrative access is protected by secure authentication mechanisms.

9. Data Retention & Account Deletion

Personal data is retained while your account remains active. Users may delete their account within the App at any time. Upon deletion request:

  • The account enters a short processing window (approximately 1–2 hours)
  • Core account data is permanently removed after this period
  • Loyalty records are erased
  • Certain minimal system logs may be retained temporarily for legal or security purposes

Deleted accounts cannot be restored after completion of the deletion process.

10. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing. Limited technical data may be processed by trusted infrastructure providers such as Firebase and AWS strictly for service operation.

11. International Data Transfers

Cloud infrastructure providers may operate secure data centers outside India. By using the App, you acknowledge and consent to such processing under appropriate safeguards.

12. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for individuals under 13 years of age. We rely on user-declared age and do not independently verify age information.
