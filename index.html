Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Madhu’s Beauty Studio operates a professional salon located in India, offering hair, skin, grooming, and wellness services. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("Madhu Beauty Studio App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Our commitment is simple: We collect only what is required to deliver salon services efficiently, securely, and professionally.

1. About Our Salon and Services

Madhu’s Beauty Studio is a single-location beauty and grooming salon. Our app allows customers to book appointments, manage profiles, track loyalty rewards, and receive service updates. The application does not function as a marketplace and does not share user data with unrelated businesses.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide appointment services:

  • Phone Number – Used for secure login and account identification.
  • Name – Displayed in booking records and used for personalization.
  • Age & Gender – To recommend and provide appropriate services.
  • Address (optional) – Only if voluntarily provided.
  • Booking Data – Selected services, appointment date/time, booking history.
  • Loyalty & Referral Data – Coins earned, redeemed, and referrals used.
  • Device & Notification Data – Firebase token for booking reminders and updates.

We do not collect financial card information within the application. Payments, if applicable, are handled at the salon or through secure channels.

3. How We Use Information

Your information is used strictly to operate salon services:

  • Account creation and authentication
  • Appointment scheduling and management
  • Sending confirmations and reminders
  • Managing cancellations and no-show policies
  • Operating loyalty and referral programs
  • Improving service quality and reliability

4. Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send:

  • Booking confirmations
  • Appointment reminders
  • Cancellation notices
  • Occasional service updates or promotional offers

You may disable notifications at any time from your device settings.

5. Third-Party Infrastructure

We use trusted service providers to operate securely:

  • Firebase Authentication – secure phone login
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging – notifications
  • Cloud infrastructure services – secure data storage

These providers may process limited technical information strictly to deliver functionality. We do not sell, trade, or rent personal data.

6. Data Storage & Security

User data is stored on secure cloud servers with reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards. Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

7. Data Retention

We retain account and booking data as long as your account remains active. Historical booking information may be retained for operational, tax, or legal compliance purposes.

8. Account Deletion

You may request account deletion within the app. Upon deletion:

  • Your personal profile information will be removed from active systems.
  • Loyalty balances will be cleared.
  • Some booking records may be retained where legally required.

9. Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed toward individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us at the email provided below.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
