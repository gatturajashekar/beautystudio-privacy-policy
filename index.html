Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Madhu’s Beauty Studio ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Madhu Beauty Studio mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Phone Number – used for authentication and account identification.
  • Name – used to personalize your profile and bookings.
  • Age and Gender – used to provide appropriate salon services.
  • Booking Information – including service selections, appointment dates, and booking history.
  • Device Identifiers – such as Firebase Instance ID for push notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To process and manage salon bookings
  • To send booking confirmations, reminders, and cancellation notifications
  • To improve our services and app functionality

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate the app, including:

  • Firebase Authentication – for secure phone number login
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging – for push notifications

These services may collect limited technical data necessary to provide their functionality. We do not sell or rent your personal data to any third party.

4. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide services. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed from our active systems.

5. Account Deletion

Users can request account deletion directly within the app. Upon deletion, we remove associated personal information and booking records unless retention is required for legal purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or misuse.

7. Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information.
