Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Madhu’s Beauty Studio ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, processes, and protects personal information through the Madhu Beauty Studio mobile application ("App").

Scope and Acceptance

By registering or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. Our services are intended for users located in India and are governed by Indian law.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Phone number (used for OTP-based authentication via Firebase Authentication)
  • Age range (optional)
  • Gender (optional)
  • Booking history and selected salon services
  • Loyalty and referral records
  • Push notification device token (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
  • Technical data such as device type, app version, and system logs for security and performance

We do not collect passwords, financial information, biometric identifiers, precise location data, or contact lists.

How We Use Information

  • Secure user authentication and account management
  • Processing salon bookings
  • Managing loyalty rewards
  • Sending booking confirmations and reminders
  • Preventing fraud and misuse
  • Maintaining system performance and security

We do not sell personal data and do not use personal data for behavioral advertising.

Third-Party Services and Data Processing

We use trusted third-party service providers to operate the App:

  • Google Firebase (Google LLC) – Authentication (phone number verification) and push notifications.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) – Cloud hosting, database storage (DynamoDB), serverless processing (Lambda), and monitoring services.

These providers process data solely on our behalf under contractual and security safeguards. Some data may be processed or stored on secure servers located outside India.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data. Personal information is shared only with service providers strictly for authentication, hosting, security, and notification delivery purposes.

Security Measures

Data is transmitted using encrypted HTTPS connections. Cloud databases use encryption at rest. Administrative access is protected through secure authentication and role-based controls. While we take reasonable steps to protect data, no system can guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only while your account remains active or as required for legitimate operational or legal purposes.

Account Deletion

Users may permanently delete their account directly within the App under Profile → Delete Account.

  • Personal profile information is permanently removed.
  • Booking and loyalty data linked to the account are deleted.
  • Limited system logs may be retained temporarily for security compliance.

Users may also request deletion by contacting us at madhubeautystudio@gmail.com.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for individuals under 13 years of age. If we become aware that personal data of a child under 13 has been collected, we will delete such data promptly.

Your Rights

Subject to applicable law including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you may request:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdrawal of consent

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a confirmed data breach affecting personal data, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
